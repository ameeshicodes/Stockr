How it works

Up and running in minutes

No training needed. If your staff can use a phone, they can use Stockr.

1

Add your products

Type in what you sell — coffee, milk, cups, syrups, anything. Set a low-stock alert level for each one.

2

Tap to count

Staff tap each item and tap + or − to update the count. Or type the exact number after a full stocktake.

3

See what's running low

Items below your threshold are flagged instantly. No more discovering you're out of oat milk at 8am.

4

Share with your team

Your whole team sees the same live counts. No more WhatsApp messages asking "how much coffee do we have?"
